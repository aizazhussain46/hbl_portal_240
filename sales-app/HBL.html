What are Mutual Funds?

Advantages of Investing in Mutual Funds

Mutual funds are an easy, convenient and affordable way of gaining access to capital markets. Each investor has a stake in the assets and earnings of the fund in proportion to the amount of their investments. The benefits of mutual funds include:

Professional Management:
The AMC appoints a team of investment professionals, assisted by qualified research analysts, to manage the portfolio of the fund in conformity with the Investment objective of the fund.

Tax Efficient Way to Save:
Tax Efficient Way to Save: Since the fund it self is not taxed if it distributes at least 90% of its unrealized income, investors earn tax free returns – at the time of redemption capital gain tax does apply but this rate becomes zero if investment is held for 1 year and above. Also, salaried and non-salaried individuals can avail tax rebate.

Diversification:
Mutual Fund portfolios usually consist of multiple investment positions/vehicles across sectors and companies, and are thus better able to mitigate risk by virtue of the their diverse portfolio. In short, a mutual fund does not put “all its eggs in one basket”. This reduces the risk an investor faces while investing in one or two securities.

 

Advantages of Investing in Mutual Funds

Liquidity:
Your money is available within 1‐3 working days. Open‐end mutual funds provide a unique opportunity to investors in liquidating their investment at a redemption price determined through the NAV methodology.

Reduction of transaction costs:
A direct investor bears all the cost of investing such as brokerage costs, and custody of security costs. When going through a fund, you have the benefit of economies of scale; the fund pay lesser costs because of larger volumes, a benefit passed on to its investor.

Convenience:
Investors can choose for automatic reinvestment or periodic distribution of the earnings of their units in the fund. Further, the investors don’t need to open CDC Account or to transfer their units through brokerage. Mutual funds also offers a wide variety of services including account statements at any time on investors request, toll free accessibility, SMS alerts dedicated investors support and designated Investment Centers for added convenience. Investors can also switch from one fund to another at any time.

Time:
Generally an investor does not have the time to actively manage portfolios. AMCs generally have a team of dedicated professionals who are relentlessly researching and analyzing stocks, visiting companies to meet management, determining business cycle ups and downs, studying macroeconomic trends, forecasting interest rate fluctuations and interpreting fiscal as well as monetary policy measures to see how they affect the movement of securities.
